How to Make Yakisoba

By: Nina Okubo

(Serves 2-3)

First... Ingredients! (Check off as you gather them!)






Now Let's Prep the Ingredients!

  1. Cut the 3 leaves of cabbage into 1 inch by 1 inch squares
  2. Cut the pork/protein into very thin 5mm slices
  3. Take 250 grams of noodles out of the package and place into a microwave safe container.

Now Let's Start Cooking!

Vegetables:

  1. Heat a drizzle of oil in a pan. Then add cabbage/vegetables. Salt and Pepper (only a little)
  2. Add 1 teaspoon of water. Stire.
  3. Once the veggies are 60% of the way cooked, place the veggies into a separate bowl/plate.

Meat/Protein:

  1. If the pan is not dry, do not oil and move onto the next step. If it is dry, add oil then continue
  2. Add meat to the pan, salt and pepper
  3. Once the meat is cooked, place the meat into a separate bowl.

Noodles:

  1. Microwave noodles in 15-30 second intervals until warm.
  2. Once the noodles are warm, separate the noodles by hand until they are all separated.
  3. Oil pan at medium heat and add the noodles to the pan.
  4. Immediately spread out and flatten the noodles evenly in the pan.
  5. Wait until the bottom of the noodles become crispy, then stire the noodles (flip the noodles if possible)
    *When stirring the noodles, take the spatula/mixing tool press down on the noodles at an angle and do a zigzag motion around the pan.
  6. If the noodles look too dry, add a teaspoon of cooking sake (cooking alcohol) to the noodles.

Put it all together!

  1. When the noodles are cooked, add the cooked vegetables and meat to the noodle pan and mix.
  2. Add 2 tablespoons of Yakisoba sauce to the pan and mix.
  3. Taste the yakisoba, if you want to add more sauce, add more until you are satisfied.
  4. When the sauce is evenly mixed with the noodles, turn off the heat and transfer to a serving bowl/plate quickly. (DON'T LET IT SIT TOO LONG!)
  5. Enjoy!
